The Problem


Dog owners don’t always have the neccessary time to properly care for their pets. From issues of health to a hectic lifestyle, owners can’t always take their loved pets on the daily walks that are needed for a dog’s well-being.



The Solution


The vision for WalkMyDog is the opportunity to cover a large market of dog-owners & provide them with a service that, with a touch of a button, helps owners with the caring of their dogs.



The Goal


Our goal is to create a mobile app that gives users access to many of our walk services, including scheduled, ASAP, and buddy walking. This app will be a solution for pet owners who need assurance that their dog will be well taken care of.

Requirements

Requsted from owners in development.


  • The app must be built so that it will function on both Apple and Android devices.
  • The app must work well with devices of all sizes as well.
  • Must be able to support thousands of simultaneous users.
  • Must use modern programming practices, so it can be easily modified later.
  • Layout must be clean and easy to use for all types of users.

Risks

Potential risks with the WalkMyDog app.


  • Dog walker could get injured and potentially sue the company.
  • Users' dogs could get hurt as well and company could be held liable for the damages.
  • Finding a developer who could use modern programming languages to fit the app could be difficult to find.
  • Having the app potentially crash on the users.
